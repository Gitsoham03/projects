Blow Moulding Machine

blow molding machine

A blow molding machine is used to manufacture hollow plastic products by inflating heated plastic into a mold cavity. It is widely utilized in producing bottles, containers, and other hollow shapes for industries like packaging, beverages, and pharmaceuticals.
